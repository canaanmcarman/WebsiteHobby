Overwatch Career

I switched from TF2 to Overwatch soon after it came out, as it took a lot of the tf2 pro players and as much as I still loved TF2 I wanted to follow the competition. I played in open division for about 1 season before joining fellow tf2 players on my first professional Overwatch team Splyce. I played for about 2-3 years before retiring due to tendinitis and switching my focus to coaching (see coaching page).

Notable teams:

  • Galeforce Esports
