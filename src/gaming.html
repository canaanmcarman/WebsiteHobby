Intro

Hello, my name is Canaan and I have been a pro gamer since 2011. I am specialized in the FPS genre and the first game I really sunk a lot of hours into was Quake Live
I never actually competed in Quake but every game I fell in love with and did end up competing in has had that same fast paced FPS feel inspired directly from Quake.

After developing tendinitis in both arms I was forced to take an early break from the player side and focused on coaching for a few years, and that is where I am at now!
